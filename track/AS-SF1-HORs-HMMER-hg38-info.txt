<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Classification and monomer-by-monomer annotation of Suprachromosomal Family 1
Alpha Satellite Higher-Order Repeats in human genome assembly.</p>
<p><b>Sequencing/Assembly name:</b> GRCh38/hg38</p>
<p><b>Assembly date:</b> 2013-12-17</p>
<p><b>GenBank accession ID:</b> <a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/assembly/GCA_000001405.15/" target="_blank">GCA_000001405.15</a></p>

<h2>Methods</h2>
Based on the phylogenetic and genomic analysis of SF1 in the hg38 genome assembly,
alpha satellite monomer sequences were established as profiles in HMMER to create
monomer-by-monomer annotation track for UCSC Genome Browser.
